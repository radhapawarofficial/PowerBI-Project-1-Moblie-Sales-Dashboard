# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard — Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing mobile phone sales performance across India, built with advanced DAX measures, map visualizations, dynamic slicers, and multi-dimensional KPI tracking.

---

## 🎯 Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of mobile sales data for top smartphone brands across Indian cities. It enables business stakeholders to track revenue performance, customer satisfaction, transaction trends, and payment behavior — all through interactive filtering by month, brand, and payment method.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* **KPI Cards:** Quick-glance metrics for Total Sales, Transactions, Average Value, and Quantity.
* **India Map Visual:** City-wise total sales plotted geographically across 15+ Indian cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Lucknow, Kolkata, etc.).
* **Brand Performance Table:** Full sales breakdown by major brands (Apple, OnePlus, Samsung, Vivo, and Xiaomi).
* **Top 3 Models:** Dynamic bar chart comparing best-selling mobile models (iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, Galaxy Note 20).
* **Customer Ratings Distribution:** Horizontal bar chart showing rating spread from 1 to 5 stars.
* **Payment Method Pie Chart:** Visual breakdown of transactions across UPI (26.36%), Cash (24.72%), Debit Card (24.69%), and Credit Card (24.22%).
* **Time-Series Trends:** * Line chart tracking daily quantity trends across the week.
  * Monthly quantity trend line tracking performance from January to December.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Full-page cross-filtering by **Month**, **Brand**, and **Payment Method**.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* **Brand Leadership:** Apple leads total sales at **₹161.6M**, followed closely by Samsung (**₹160M**) and OnePlus (**₹153.7M**).
* **Consumer Behavior:** Saturday records the highest daily quantity, with a consistent downward trend toward Wednesday, strongly suggesting weekend-driven purchasing behavior.
* **Payment Preferences:** Transactions are nearly equally distributed across UPI, Cash, Debit Card, and Credit Card (~25% each), indicating a balanced multi-channel payment ecosystem.
* **Customer Satisfaction:** Customer ratings heavily skew toward 5 stars, followed by 4 stars, showing a highly positive overall product satisfaction signal.
* **Seasonality:** June and July experience a distinct peak in monthly quantity, pointing toward a mid-year demand spike potentially driven by summer sale seasons.
* **Emerging Markets:** Tier-2 cities like Lucknow and Gorakhpur appear prominently on the sales map, highlighting them as high-potential emerging markets for targeted marketing.

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used

* **Power BI Desktop:** Dashboard design, canvas layout, and visual formatting.
* **DAX Formulas:** Developed robust measures using `SUM`, `AVERAGE`, `COUNT`, and `CALCULATE`.
* **Power Query:** Applied data transformation, type formatting, and cleaning procedures on raw transaction records.
* **Data Modeling:** Integrated map visualization via Bing Maps and mapped relational cross-filtering.
* **UI/UX Styling:** Built a customized Motorola-branded theme using a clean, professional dark blue and white color palette with optimal whitespace preservation.
